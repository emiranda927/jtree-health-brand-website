Our programs

Care that meets your teen where they are.

Three levels of structured outpatient support, designed around school schedules and family life. No inpatient stay required.

Mon–Fri · 9 am – 3 pm

Partial hospitalization (PHP)

Five days a week of group, individual, and family work for teens needing the most support. Daily structure with school accommodations.

Intensive outpatient (IOP)

After-school care that lets your teen stay enrolled at school. Skills group, individual therapy, family sessions on a regular cadence.

How it works

Three steps. We do the heavy lifting.

1

Tell us a little

Fill out the inquiry form or call. Two minutes, no clinical detail required.

2

A real conversation

An admissions clinician calls you within one business day. We listen. No high-pressure sales.

3

A plan, together

If we're the right fit, we set a start date and verify your insurance. If we aren't, we'll point you to who is.
