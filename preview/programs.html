PHP Program

Full-day care. Home each evening.

PHP is a structured day program — 5 days a week, 9am to 3pm — for teens who need intensive mental health care but can go home each evening. Teens receive 30 hours of clinical care per week, with academic support built in.

Your teen may be ready for PHP if:

  • Weekly therapy alone isn't making a difference
  • They've been discharged from inpatient and need step-down support
  • They're in crisis but safe enough to come home each evening
  • School attendance has been severely impacted
  • Safety concerns are present but manageable in a structured setting

We work with teens experiencing:

Anxiety Depression OCD ADHD Trauma / PTSD Self-Harm Suicidal Ideation Co-occurring Disorders

Covered by most major insurance plans. We're in-network with BCBS, Cigna, Aetna, UHC, and Tricare — and we verify your benefits before you start, so there are no surprises.

IOP Program

After school. Life stays intact.

Three afternoons a week, 3–6 p.m. Teens stay in school, stay at home, and stay connected to their lives — while getting the intensive support they actually need.

Your teen may be ready for IOP if:

  • Their current therapist recommends a higher level of care
  • They're stepping down from PHP and need continued structure
  • Symptoms are present but school attendance is manageable
  • They need intensive support but they're ready to stay in school

Integrated Psychiatric Care

Medication Management

For teens who benefit from psychiatric evaluation and medication support. Our psychiatrists are embedded in the treatment team — not a separate referral.

Board-Certified Psychiatrists

Child and adolescent psychiatrists with deep experience in teen mental health, trauma-informed care, and complex presentations.

Pharmacogenomic Testing

Genetic testing that identifies how your teen's body metabolizes specific medications — removing the guesswork from prescribing decisions.

Coordinated with Therapy

Psychiatric and therapy teams meet weekly to align on treatment goals. Your teen's care plan is genuinely integrated, not siloed.

Ongoing Monitoring

As your teen progresses, dosing is reviewed and adjusted carefully — always with your input. No changes happen without your knowledge.
