Our Programs

Two programs.
One team.
Zero waitlists.

PHP for full-day structured care. IOP for after-school intensive support. Both evidence-based. Both built for how teens actually live.

Full Day

Partial Hospitalization
(PHP)

Five days a week, 9 a.m. – 3 p.m. Structured daily care for teens who need more than therapy — and can still go home each night.

  • Group + individual therapy daily
  • Family session every week
  • Academic liaison on staff
  • Lunch + snacks provided
Learn About PHP
After School

Intensive Outpatient
(IOP)

Three afternoons a week, 3 – 6 p.m. Teens stay in school, stay at home, and get intensive therapeutic support.

  • Group therapy core
  • Individual session weekly
  • Family session biweekly
  • Dinner provided
Learn About IOP
Add-on

Medication Management

Board-certified psychiatrist for teens who benefit from pharmacological support alongside therapy.

  • Adolescent-focused prescribing
  • Coordinates with your pediatrician
  • In-network with major plans
  • Monthly follow-ups
